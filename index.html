About The Gym

Built for people who train like it matters.

999 Intense Gym & Nutrition Bar spans two spacious floors on Old Bridge Road, purpose-built with imported strength and cardio machines, a dedicated functional training zone, and a fully stocked nutrition store on-site. Every rep here is supervised by certified trainers who know the difference between motion and progress.

We built this gym around comfort as much as intensity — showers, sauna, child care, and gender-neutral washrooms mean the only thing you have to think about is your workout. It's a premium experience, priced for a real Srikakulam gym.
